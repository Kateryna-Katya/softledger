The Garden

A Living Ledger

This is not a race to fill every tile.
Each plant affects its surroundings in subtle ways.
Some plants provide shade.
Some retain moisture.
Some require open space to thrive.
If you mess up the balance, everything starts dying off pretty quickly.
If things get out of whack, your garden's gonna suffer.

How It Works

How to Play

  • Choose a plant from your available selection.
  • Place it on the garden grid.
  • Observe how it affects sunlight, water, and nearby space.
  • Keep everything balanced or your plants start dying off.

There are no timers.
Only steady, thoughtful decisions.
