How It Works

How to Play

  • Choose a plant from your available selection.
  • Place it on the garden grid.
  • Observe how it affects sunlight, water, and nearby space.
  • Keep everything balanced or your plants start dying off.

There are no timers.
Only steady, thoughtful decisions.

Garden Rules

Rules of Balance

  • Sunlight Balance

    Each plant requires a specific amount of sunlight.
    Too much shade slows growth.
    Too much exposure causes dryness and instability.
    Tall plants cast shade, while smaller plants depend on partial light. Where you put stuff really matters for whether it lives or dies.

  • Water Distribution

    Water spreads across adjacent tiles.
    Moisture-loving plants increase hydration levels nearby.
    Excess water creates imbalance and weakens the ecosystem.
    Put things in the right spots and your soil stays happy.

  • Space & Proximity

    Some plants thrive when clustered.
    Others need open breathing room.
    Overcrowding reduces stability.
    Give plants the right amount of room and everything works better together.

  • Stability Meter

    There's this meter that shows you how well your garden's doing balance-wise.
    When sunlight, water, and space remain aligned, stability increases.
    If imbalance grows too large, plant health declines.

  • Scoring

    Higher scores are earned by:

    • Maintaining equilibrium
    • Using minimal plant placements
    • Avoiding instability
    • Achieving long-term sustainability

    You'll get higher scores if your garden looks neat and you've thought about where everything goes.
